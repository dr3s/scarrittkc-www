Scare-it Halloween

Get the pun… "Scarritt Neighborhood" hosts SCARE-IT Halloween?? Yes, we host a huge and we mean HUGE Halloween event that brings in about 7,500 people! Our residents lining Gladstone Boulevard from the Museum down to Mersington Ave, along with lots of volunteers, make the night special by going ALL OUT on decorations and costumes for themselves and their houses. We’ve had a 20 foot pirate ship to a "real" car wreck to the full blown cast of The Wizard of Oz. We also have things like inflatables, slides and obstacle coursE and moonwalks, live chalk artists, haunted houses, train rides and police on horseback (always a hit)!

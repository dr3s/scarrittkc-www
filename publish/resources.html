Affordability & Home Ownership Incentives

http://edckc.com/agencies-partners/land-clearance-for-redevelopment-authority/homeowners/

Thanks to the help of LISC, we created the Scarritt Renaissance Urban Renewal Plan, which gives us a 10 year tax abatement if you put in $5,000 improvements on your home/grounds. The process is easy and streamlined. This is of particular value because KC’s property taxes will likely be increasing incrementally over the coming years.

LISC

LISC is sponsoring Scarritt Renaissance as part of their "Neighborhood Now" target neighborhoods. LISC, a national urban neighborhood redevelopment association that we are so fortunate to have FUND OUR community organizer and PROVIDE THE assistance in finding the grants we need to do the projects we want.

Excellent School Options

Yes, you read correctly. Live in KC, MO school district and still give your child an outstanding education, with a little research and choice making.

Accredited schools right in our own neighborhood:

  • (Private) Holy Cross - Very passionate leadership and excellent student results. Grades PK-8
  • (Public) Scuola Vita Nuova Charter School - SVN is a safe collaborative community of learners who think independently, work creatively, and contribute positively to the world. K-8th grade

FREE excellent public schools just outside the neighborhood:

  • Academie Lafayette - AL is of the top ten public schools in the state of Missouri and all classes are in FRENCH! Your child gets a great education and will be bilingual. Grades K-8.
  • (Public) KIPP Endeavor Academy - KEA was founded in 2007 based on the belief that all students can and will learn when provided with a quality education. KIPP Endeavor Academy is located in the heart of the urban core of Kansas City, drawing students from the entire Kansas City Missouri School District. We are a free, open-enrollment charter school serving over 250 middle school students in grades 5-8
  • Frontier Charter - Sponsored by UMKC, grades K-12 with a very impressive 99.9% graduation rate.
  • Border Star Montessori - The founder of Google has said that you can’t understand his enterprise if you don’t understand Montessori education. Montessori sets out to create lifetime learners. Grade PK- 6th grade
  • The Ewing Marion Kauffman School - Mission is to "create college graduates". Grades 5-12.
  • Kauffman Scholars - Opportunity for your child to become an elite "Kauffman Scholar"
  • Paseo Academy of Fine and Performing Arts - High School
  • Lincoln Prep - Grades 6-12
